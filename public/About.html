About us

WHAT IS Ghar Milega?

Ghar Milega is the new Launch for property in Karachi and is among the newest Web which only deals with Property of Karachi. It was founded in 2020 and has since revolutionised the real estate industry of Karachi by connecting buyers and sellers online in a highly convenient way, making it a household name among Pakistanis around the world. With over 40,000 new listings each month, and over 5.5 million monthly users,it is the pioneering property portal of Karachi, with more than 15 agencies registered yet.

How did it all start?

Ghar Milega was the brainchild of two British-Pakistani entrepreneur brothers, Zeeshan Ali Khan and Imran Ali Khan. Their mission was to make Pakistani real estate accessible and convenient for everyone. Together, they proceeded to lead Ghar Milega from a small start-up to the premier real estate entity of Karachi. In 2020, French property portal veteran Gilles Blanchard, the co-founder of France’s largest portal Seloger.com, came on board as an angel investor and also took up the role of Chairman. Then, in 2020, two venture capital firms, Singapore-based Catcha Group and Malaysia-based Frontier Digital Ventures, invested in Ghar Milega, paving the way towards rapid expansion. A further two rounds of major investment were closed in late 2020 and early 2019, bringing in a total of $12 million. Then, in 2020, Ghar Milega’s parent company, the Emerging Markets Property Group (EMPG) raised $100 million. This round was led by KCK Group, with Exor Seeds, a highly distinguished company, participating. Ghar Milega has since grown from strength to strength, with over 3,000 employees and presence in over 30 cities around Pakistan, and offices in 25.

What do we do?

Ghar Milega connects buyers with sellers and tenants with landlords for highly user-friendly real estate experience. The extensive listings and projects on offer provide something for everyone when it comes to property.

Ghar Expos

The company is also the pioneer of large-scale real estate events and frequently organizes expos both locally and internationally. It holds widely attended expos in Karachi, it has also expanded its events to Dubai, with the Pakistan Property Show. An astounding 20,000 people visit Ghar Expos every year, and can explore property options with over 100 exhibitors.
